Let’s Learn Together#

Welcome to “Let’s Learn Together”, your gateway to the thrilling universe of cybersecurity challenges and write-ups from TryHackMe(THM), Hack The Box(HTB), and PortSwigger’s Web Security Academy.

In this blog, we will explore a myriad of exciting topics, ranging from hands-on hacking experiences and in-depth analysis of Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges to web application security and penetration testing tutorials. Our primary focus will be on the platforms that have become the go-to sources for cybersecurity practitioners and enthusiasts.

What to Expect#

  1. THM Write-ups: TryHackMe is a renowned platform for hands-on cybersecurity training, featuring an array of CTFs, vulnerable machines, and various labs. We’ll provide you with detailed write-ups and walkthroughs of some of the most captivating challenges, helping you understand the methodologies, tools, and techniques used by cybersecurity professionals.

  2. HTB Adventures: Hack The Box is a prominent platform that offers realistic hacking scenarios, ranging from beginner-friendly to expert-level challenges. We’ll guide you through the process of solving these puzzles, enhancing your skills and knowledge in the field.

  3. PortSwigger’s Web Security Academy: PortSwigger’s Web Security Academy is a treasure trove of web application security knowledge. We’ll explore the intricacies of web vulnerabilities, such as Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), SQL Injection, and more, with practical examples and hands-on tutorials.
